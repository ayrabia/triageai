The Problem

Specialty clinic staff spend hours every day clicking through hundreds of incoming fax referrals. These documents are named only by fax number, with no way to identify which patients need urgent care. Critical referrals get buried in backlogs of thousands, leading to delayed treatment, lost revenue, and patient safety risks.

“There is no fast way to sort — documents are named by fax number so you have to click every single one.”

— Referral Coordinator, ENT Clinic (Design Partner #1)
